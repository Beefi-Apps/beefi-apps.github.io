Contact

Before You Contact Us

  • Make sure Pomates is updated to the latest available version.
  • Check that notifications are enabled if timer completion alerts are not appearing.
  • For app blocking, check that Screen Time permission is enabled in Pomates and iOS settings.
  • For Apple Watch issues, confirm the Pomates watch app is installed and paired with the iPhone app.
